Keto Nuggets

ARE CHICKEN NUGGETS KETO?

Unfortunately, none of the store-bought keto nuggets or homemade classic nugget recipes are keto-approved. All manufactured nugget recipes include a lot of carbs in the wheat flour and breadcrumb coating. Plus, youd be surprised by the other additives that go in the coating, like sugar or vegetable oils, that arenâ€™t keto either.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Almond flour or almond meal
  • Water and vinegar
  • Grated parmesan
  • Egg and heavy cream
  • Salt
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Oregano
  • Paprika
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken thighs into small nugget-shaped pieces.
  2. Place the eggs into a mixing bowl and whisk. Add the chicken to the eggs.
  3. In another bowl, add the protein powder, parmesan, salt, garlic, paprika, and pepper. Mix.
  4. Bread each piece of chicken with the protein mixture and shake off the excess.
  5. Place a large nonstick frying pan over medium heat and add the lard. Cook the nuggets for 3-5 minutes on each side, until golden brown and cooked through. You might need to cook them in batches, depending on the size of your frying pan.
  6. In s small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise and sweet chili sauce.
  7. Serve the Keto Nuggets immediately with a side of Keto Sweet Chili Mayonnaise.
